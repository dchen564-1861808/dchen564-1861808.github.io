Random Anime/Film thoughts (Paragraphs w/ different fonts)

I wonder what it's like to fly or be super mobile like any superheros. Marvel movies like Spider-Man makes me excited about new films that are being teased. I think I'd prefer to have spells like Dr. Strange and Wong if I were in a Marvel Universe.

I don't sleep enough. Sometimes I wonder if it would be a worthy ability to not have to sleep because sometimes sleepless nights feel so lonely. I wish I could live without having to sleep, but I feel like this sentiment is too close to something I've seen out of Fullmetal Alchemist.
